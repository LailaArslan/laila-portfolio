About

Designing with clarity, research & the right tools.

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in visual design and hands-on experience designing customer-facing digital platforms. My work sits at the intersection of user understanding and product clarity — taking services that are often complex and fragmented, and shaping them into experiences that feel simple and purposeful.

I'm interested in how AI can support faster research, smarter prototyping, and more efficient design workflows — helping teams move from insight to tested solutions without losing the human understanding that makes good UX work.

Process

How AI fits into my design process

I use AI as a thinking partner and accelerator — not a replacement for user understanding. Here's where it adds real value across my workflow.

01

Discovery & Framing

Turning a fragmented brief into sharp research questions before any pixels — mapping stakeholders, goals and the real problem to solve.

02

Research Synthesis

AI-assisted clustering of interview notes, thematic analysis across sessions, and faster identification of patterns and pain points.

03

Concept Exploration

Rapidly generating and stress-testing design directions, content structures and information-architecture options.

04

Prototyping

Using AI to build interactive prototypes faster — moving from a written brief to a testable artifact in days, not weeks.

05

Usability Testing

Drafting test scripts, generating edge-case scenarios and synthesizing post-test findings into clear next steps.
