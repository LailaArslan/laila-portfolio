About

Designing with clarity, research, and the right tools.

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in visual design and hands-on experience designing customer-facing digital platforms. My work sits at the intersection of user understanding and product clarity — taking services that are often complex and fragmented, and shaping them into experiences that feel simple and purposeful.

I'm interested in how AI can support faster research, smarter prototyping, and more efficient product design workflows — helping teams move from insight to tested solutions without losing the human understanding that makes good UX work.

Process

How AI fits into my design process

I use AI as a thinking partner and accelerator — not a replacement for user understanding. Here's where it adds real value in my workflow.

01

Brief & Discovery

Using AI to analyze briefs, identify gaps in the framing, and surface the right questions before user research begins.

02

Research Synthesis

AI-assisted clustering of interview notes, thematic analysis across sessions, and faster identification of patterns — without losing qualitative nuance.

03

Concept Exploration

Rapidly generating and stress-testing design directions, content structures, and IA options — exploring more ground in less time.

04

Prototyping

Using AI to build interactive prototypes faster. Moving from a written brief to a testable artifact in days — this portfolio being one example of that workflow.

05

Usability Testing

Drafting test scripts, generating edge-case scenarios, and synthesizing post-test findings to accelerate the iteration cycle.
